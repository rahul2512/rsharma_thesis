Rahul Sharma hails from Loharu, a beautiful and historic town in the north of India, and was born in 1992.
He received his integrated bachelor's and master's degree in Chemistry from the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee.
During his undergraduate studies, he had the opportunity to work on exciting projects studying biomolecules using molecular simulations and developed an interest in computational chemistry.
In June 2018, he started his doctoral studies under the supervision of Prof. John H. Maddocks at EPFL.
