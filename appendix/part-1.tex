\chapter{Ideal atoms coordinates in Tsukuba convention} \label[appendix]{app1}
This chapter lists the ideal coordinates for bases and phosphates used to fit the frames to coarse-grain NAs. 
The ideal coordinates for standard bases are as per Tsukuba convention~\cite{tsukuba} and phosphate is approximated as  tetrahedron as provided in \cref{a1:t1}
For non-standard bases, 5-methylated Cytosine and 5-hydroxylmethylated Cytosine, Cytosine coordinates are taken.
